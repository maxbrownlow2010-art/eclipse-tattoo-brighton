About the Studio

An independent
studio with soul.

Eclipse has been a fixture of Brighton's North Laine for years — a creative space where artists and clients collaborate to make permanent art. Whether you're after delicate fine line work or bold traditional pieces, our collective brings your vision to life.

Our piercing suite operates to the highest safety standards, with a wide selection of implant-grade jewellery available in studio.

How to Book

For custom tattoos, contact your chosen artist directly via Instagram. Walk-ins accepted subject to availability, 7 days a week.

01
Custom Tattoos

DM your chosen artist on Instagram to discuss your idea, placement, and sizing. They'll walk you through the process and arrange a session.

02
Walk-Ins

Come in any day of the week. Subject to artist availability. Smaller pieces and flash designs welcome without an appointment.

03
Piercing

Walk in or message us on Instagram. No appointment necessary for most piercings — just come in and our piercer will look after you.
